The Portal & The Builder

Here, we explore the philosophy of Coddy and meet the builder. "I don’t replace myself with AI — I amplify myself."

Coddy's Core Protocols

Coddy retains context from our entire loop, not just the last prompt. It understands the full history of the project.

It reads, understands, and updates our `roadmap.md` file, keeping development aligned with our goals.

By analyzing the codebase, Coddy identifies and suggests reusable patterns, improving consistency and quality.

Coddy can initiate new projects from a single idea, performing the "genesis ritual" to create the foundational file structure and boilerplate code.
